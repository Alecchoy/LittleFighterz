How to Play
Player 1 Controls
  • 1. Use "WASD" to move up, down, left, right
  • 2. 'F' is teleport
  • 3. 'G' is basic attack
Player 2 Controls
  • 1. Use the arrow keys to move up, down, left, right
  • 2. ',' is teleport
  • 3. '.' is basic attack
